Groups

Two groups of nine teams.	
Teams play every other team in their group in a best-of-two.	
Top four teams in each group advance to Upper Bracket of the Main Event.	
Teams in 5-8 place in each group advance to Lower Bracket of the Main Event.	
Bottom team from each group is eliminated.
